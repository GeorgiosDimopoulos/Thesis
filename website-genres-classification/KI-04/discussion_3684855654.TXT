 Fried Green Tomatoes with White Cheddar Grits    Track this topic |  Email this topic |  Print this topic

ADDvanced Forum Guide Group: Members Posts: 1712 Member No.: 771 Joined: 13-October 03 	Fried Green Tomatoes with White Cheddar GritsINGREDIENTS:Fried Green Tomatoes1 cup panko bread crumbs1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper1/2 teaspoon cumin2 teaspoons chili powder3 each green tomatoesFlour (as needed)1 cup buttermilkWhite Cheddar Grits1 cup vegetable stock3 ounces Quaker quick grits1/2 tablespoon roasted corn1/4 pound white cheddar cheese, gratedSalt and white pepper to taste1/8 cup heavy cream3/8 tablespoon butterTomatillo Salsa1/3 pound tomatillos, peeled2/3 ounce garlic, whole and peeled1/3 bunch cilantro (stems removed)2/3 each jalapeno chile peppers, de-seeded2 ounces Roma tomato1/3 pound tomato1/8 teaspoon kosher salt1/8 teaspoon sugar DIRECTIONS:To prepare the tomatoes, slice tomatoes 1/2-inch thick. Dredge in flour and soak in buttermilk for five minutes. Set aside. Mix the dry ingredients together and roll the tomatoes in the mixture. Fry until golden brown in clarified butter. Serve with white cheddar grits and tomatillo salsa.For the grits, heat the stock, then add remaining ingredients. Simmer to desired consistency (creamy).To prepare the salsa, pull the skins off the tomatillos and simmer in water for 10 minutes until tender. Puree with remaining ingredients.Recipe courtesy: Chef Michael Cross, Bacchanal Bistro and Wine Bar http://www.kxtv10.com -------------------- Live the question as well as the answer.
