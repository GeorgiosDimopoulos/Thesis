Christmas Cookies for Your Pup! Doggie Biscuits 1 cup cornmeal 3 1/2 cups whole-wheat flour 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder 4 tablespoons instant soup stock mix(beef, chicken or vegetable) 4 tablespoons bacon bits 3/4 cup vegetable oil 1 1/3 cup water (approx) Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Blend all ingredients thoroughly. Roll mixture 1/4 inch thick onto floured cutting board or other smooth surface. Cut to desired shapes with cookies cutters or a knife. Be creative! Bake for approx 35 - 45 minutes, basting lightly with meat or bacon drippings. Cool thoroughly before serving.
