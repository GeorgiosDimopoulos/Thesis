''Stitch! The Movie'' DVD	 
                     
            	      
      	 The high-spirited adventures of ''Lilo & Stitch'' continue in ''Stitch! The Movie,'' premiering only on Disney DVD and VHS. Stitch is one of a kind, but he soon discovers that he's not alone when he meets the rest of his family -- the other 625 alien experiments. It's nonstop thrills and even more action as Stitch, Lilo and their ''adopted'' aunt and uncle, Pleakley and Jumba, set out on a mission to rescue the other experiments. With more humor, action and adventure, and featuring new music -- including more Elvis songs -- families will fall in love with Stitch in this fun-filled all-new movie. English, French and Spanish language tracks. Dolby Digital 5.1 Surround Sound. Not rated. 

DVD Bonus Features

* Animated menus with Stitch as your guide
* Trivia Challenge -- See the evil Dr. Hamsterviel's production of ''The Making of Stitch!'' and then take his Trivia Pop Quiz
* Experiment Finder -- Help Lilo and Stitch find the infernal experiments
* Experiment Gallery -- Meet more aliens and discover their powers
* ''Aloha, E Komo Mai'' performed by Jump 5 music video
