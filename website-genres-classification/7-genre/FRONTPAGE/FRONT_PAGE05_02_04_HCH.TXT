Schizophrenic convicted murderer gets 60-day reprieve
A federal judge on Wednesday granted an execution stay for a schizophrenic murderer, reopening the debate over how Texas treats mentally ill violent offenders. Scott Panetti was granted a 60-day stay a day before he was to be executed for the 1992 shooting deaths of his estranged wife's parents.
