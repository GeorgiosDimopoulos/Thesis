Clara Harris convicted of murder 
Jurors convicted Clara Harris of murder today for running over her husband outside a Clear Lake-area hotel on July 24 after finding him with his mistress. Jurors now must consider her punishment. They could send her to prison for life, but they could also give her probation. With video.
