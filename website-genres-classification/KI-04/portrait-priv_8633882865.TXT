Curriculum vitae 

I studied mathematics (Abschluss: Sehr Gut) with a minor in computer science (Abschluss: 1.0) at the University of Mannheim. Master's thesis An algorithm for spline approximation via Gauss-Transformation. I did my Ph.D. thesis at Lehrstuhl Praktische Informatik IV (Practical Computer Science IV). Since then I am the Chief Cryptographer of the cryptolabs Amsterdam. In May 2002 I have joined the reseach group of Prof. Andrew S. Tanenbaum at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. I am also a longtime member of the Chaos Computer Club.
